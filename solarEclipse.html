Solar Eclipse

Explanation

Solar Eclipses happen about every 18 months.

These happen when the moon goes in front of the sun.

This 2017 Eclipse is the first one to go across the USA in 38 years.

Tips:

Never look straight into the sun.

Solar Eclipse glasses can be purchased so you can safely look into the sun.

Make sure to purchase them early, they'll sell out!

There are only certain places where you can experience totality - these will be crowded.

Have fun while staying safe!
